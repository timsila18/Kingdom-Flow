# Prompt 6 - Discipleship, Programmes and Church Academy

Prompt 6 adds a configurable church-learning system for foundation classes, discipleship, Bible school, ministry training, leadership development, mentorship and certificates.

Principles:

- churches control programme names, curriculum, trainers, fees, assessments, certificates and progression;
- KingdomFlow does not enforce doctrine, theology, interpretation or denominational structure;
- paying a fee never guarantees spiritual growth, salvation, ministry appointment, divine favour or spiritual authority;
- paid programmes disclose church fee, technology fee, provider cost, refund policy and scholarship policy;
- free church programmes have a zero KingdomFlow technology fee;
- programme progress supports care and planning, not faith ranking.

Implemented foundations:

- programme types, programmes, versions, modules, lessons, materials, sessions and cohorts;
- trainer assignments and trainer qualification placeholders;
- applications, eligibility explanations, enrolments, waitlist and new-convert progression;
- scholarships with restricted hardship reason visibility;
- transparent fee disclosure, technology-fee bands and church payment destinations;
- programme payments, manual verification and refund foundations;
- class attendance, assignments, assessments, grading, moderation and completion rules;
- certificates with public QR/code verification and no private learner contact data;
- graduation, leadership pathways and mentorship foundations;
- learner portal, trainer workspace, coordinator dashboard, catalogue and reports;
- activity signals for future subscription analytics.

Reserved for later prompts: live payment adapters, full LMS media delivery, real PDF rendering/signature asset storage, finance posting and communications provider delivery.
