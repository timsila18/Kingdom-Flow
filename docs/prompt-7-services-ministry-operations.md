# Prompt 7 - Services, Worship and Ministry Operations

Prompt 7 adds the church-service operations layer for Sunday services, worship planning, departments, volunteers, rosters, technical readiness, protocol, incidents and post-service reporting.

Principles:

- church services remain configurable by branch, service type, department and local workflow;
- worship, choir, media, ushers, protocol, hospitality, security, parking and announcements are operational modules, not spiritual rankings;
- livestream and communications features are adapter-ready but do not fake external delivery;
- volunteer applications, training and availability support human review, not automatic appointment;
- safeguarding and sensitive incidents route to protected care workflows and stay out of ordinary reports.

Implemented foundations:

- service types, recurring schedules, generated service instances, templates and order-of-service items;
- sermons, series metadata, public summaries and restricted preparation notes;
- worship sets, song library, music teams, choir/band readiness and rehearsals;
- departments, hierarchy protection, leaders, responsibilities and department dashboards;
- volunteer profiles, applications, eligibility checks, availability and volunteer portal;
- roster assignments, assisted suggestions, conflict detection, confirmations, replacements and check-ins;
- equipment checklists, technical cues, media requests and livestream planning placeholders;
- protocol, hospitality, security, parking and announcement workflow surfaces;
- service reports, post-service actions, attendance summary handoff fields and incident redaction;
- activity-ready dashboards for coordinators, departments, volunteers and leadership.

Reserved for later prompts: real livestream provider adapters, communications sending, asset inventory deduction, payment/finance postings and cross-module automation delivery.
