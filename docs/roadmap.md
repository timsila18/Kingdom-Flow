# Module Roadmap

Prompt 1 fully implements foundations for home, church profile, organizational structure, branches, users, roles, terminology, branding, subscription overview, settings and audit.

Reserved for later prompts: members and households, visitors and new converts, follow-up, pastoral care, prayer, testimonies, cells, discipleship, services, volunteers, events, missions, giving, finance, procurement, accounting, HR, payroll, assets, transport, facilities, media, communications, reports, AI Ministry Copilot, live payment workflows and external integrations.

Future modules must extend the existing tenant, authorization, audit and RLS foundations rather than bypassing them.
