# Prompt 11: Member App, Communication and Digital Ministry

Prompt 11 adds the member-facing digital companion and communication ecosystem.

## Implemented

- Member portal with mobile-first navigation, personalized home, journey, church, fellowship, sermons, live, devotionals, Bible plans, prayer, giving, messages, announcements, appointments, family, documents, profile, privacy and help surfaces.
- Digital membership cards with minimum-necessary QR scan output.
- Next-steps engine tied to actual lifecycle records, programmes and fellowship placement.
- Sermon library, series, media, scriptures, captions/transcript foundations, private sermon notes, bookmarks and progress.
- Livestream foundation with configured providers and no secret stream-key exposure.
- Devotionals and Bible reading plans with resume/restart and no missed-day shaming.
- Prayer, testimony and pastoral appointment workflows that avoid exposing sensitive details.
- Announcement, communication preference, delivery evidence, provider, notification center and emergency broadcast foundations.
- Native messaging, conversations, moderation, blocking/reporting and adult-minor safeguards.
- Optional Solco integration scaffold with adapter status and native fallback. Mock development mode is clearly marked and never treated as production delivery.
- AI Ministry Copilot foundations with tenant policy, permitted modules, usage/cost controls, source records, human review and safety events.
- PWA manifest and safe installable shell.

## Boundaries

- No spiritual scores or leaderboards.
- No fake delivery confirmation.
- No fake Solco API result.
- No fake AI citations.
- No adult-minor private messaging outside approved supervised channels.
- No confidential prayer, counselling, safeguarding, giving, payroll or private sermon-note leakage.
- No AI divine claims or autonomous pastoral action.

## Reserved For Prompt 12

Prompt 12 should extend analytics and church-growth intelligence using safe aggregate signals from Prompt 11 without creating invasive surveillance or spiritual rankings.
