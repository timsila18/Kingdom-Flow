# Prompt 8 - Events, Outreach, Missions, Children, Youth and Campus Ministry

Prompt 8 adds a secure event and ministry-participation layer for conferences, camps, children activities, outreach campaigns, mission trips, youth ministry and campus fellowships.

Principles:

- events reuse central tenants, branches, people, households, guardians, volunteers, programmes, care and permissions;
- public event pages expose only approved content;
- registrations, check-in, transport, accommodation and reports use real workflow states, not fake completion;
- children, teens and vulnerable participants require guardian consent, pickup controls and restricted incident handling;
- outreach contact capture uses consent and never treats people as sales leads;
- mission documents remain restricted and never leak into general reports.

Implemented foundations:

- event types, event register, builder workflow, planning-team assignments and event sessions;
- registration categories, registrations, fees, tickets, QR pass records, badges and check-in;
- speaker, venue, room, meal, accommodation and transport planning foundations;
- transport manifests, child pickup validation, failed pickup attempts and safeguarding ratio checks;
- outreach campaigns, outreach contacts, duplicate detection and Prompt 3 follow-up handoff metadata;
- mission trips, applications, team readiness and restricted mission documents;
- children classes, child check-in/pickup, authorized pickup adults and child incident redaction;
- teen, youth and campus ministry records with transitions that preserve central people profiles;
- coordinator, events, children, youth, campus and report dashboards.

Reserved for later prompts: live payment adapters, actual refunds, procurement/stock accounting, fleet/facility management, external communication delivery and full finance posting.
